Pradnya & Pankaj : Couple Shoot in Mumbai

Couple Shoot

By Esha G.

“Love bears all things and makes them great.”


Pradnya and Pankaj wanted their pre-wedding shoot to be done in South Bombay and Alibaug. Mumbai is undoubtedly one of the most admirable pre-wedding shoots locations in India. We seized their wonderful memories by capturing every bit of the details.

This charming maharashtrian couple met each other at work and fell in the depths of love. After spending some time together they decided to start a brand new chapter in their lives, together.

We started our journey early in the morning for South Bombay. South Bombay can be very crowded in the afternoon, so it is best visited in the morning hours. We captured some amazing shots at the most iconic landmarks of the city like the Gateway of India, Taj Hotel, and Marine Drive, also known as the queen’s necklace. 

A glimpse of this happy couple’s amazing chemistry can be seen through the pictures. After the shoot at South Bombay, we took a ferry to Alibaug. Alibaug is a little coastal region tucked away in Raigad District of Maharashtra. It took us around an hour to reach our next destination. Alibaug is popularly known for its numerous beaches, that are only a few minute drives away from each other. The sandy beaches, clean unpolluted air, forts and temples here are every photographer’s paradise. We made the most out of the gorgeous sunset by clicking some lovely pictures of this charming couple.

Directing these two love-birds in this magical destination felt effortless. It was a fun-filled photo-shoot altogether. It was a pleasure to help them capture some beautiful and fun memories of their life. Here’s how are this magical shoot turned out to be!
